Our Story

From a passionate dream in 2021 to Naivasha's beloved artisanal bakery - discover the journey that makes every bite special

Naivasha
2021
Founded

A Dream Born in the Kitchen

It all started with a simple dream and a tiny rented stall in Naivasha’s bustling market. After years of seeing locals rely on factory bread, the vision grew stronger when neighbors couldn’t stop praising the taste of freshly baked loaves made during quiet weekends.

“I realized that baking wasn’t just about producing food to sell,” the founder reflects. “It was about creating joy, bringing people together, and keeping the spirit of honest baking alive in a fast-changing world.”

What began as a modest start-up with one oven has grown into Naivasha’s trusted bakery, but the heart remains unchanged – every creation is made with care, resilience, and respect for tradition.

From Dough to Delight

Step into the world of Kahaki Bakers, where every creation begins with the simple touch of flour and water and grows into something extraordinary. From the early morning mixing of dough to the careful shaping, patient rising, and golden baking, each stage is guided by skill and passion. The journey doesn’t end there — our team adds the finishing touches, from delicate decoration on cakes to the warm packaging of fresh loaves still carrying the scent of the oven.

It’s more than a process; it’s a story of time, tradition, and heart. At Kahaki Bakers, every bite you enjoy has passed through hands that care, ovens that nurture, and a community that inspires. This is what makes us special — bread, cakes, and pastries crafted not just to fill, but to delight.

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision, from ingredient sourcing to community involvement

Local Partnerships

From flour to flavor, we partner with Naivasha’s millers, farmers, and local vendors to ensure every item reflects freshness, trust, and community spirit

Sustainability

Eco-friendly packaging, minimal waste practices, and energy-efficient equipment reflect our commitment to environmental responsibility.

Community First

From supporting local markets to sharing baking skills, we believe in giving back to the Naivasha community that inspires us.

Our Baking Philosophy

Time & Patience

Our sourdough ferments for 72 hours, allowing complex flavors to develop naturally. Good things take time, and we never rush the process.

Seasonal Ingredients

We rotate our menu based on seasonal availability, ensuring peak freshness and supporting local agricultural cycles.

Handcrafted Excellence

Every item receives individual attention. From hand-shaping bread to personally decorating each cake, craftsmanship is our signature.
