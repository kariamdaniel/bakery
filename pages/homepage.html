Our Story

Born from a passion for bringing traditional European baking techniques to Nairobi's vibrant culinary scene, Sweet Crumbs began as a dream in our founder's kitchen in 2019.

Today, we're proud to serve Westlands and beyond with handcrafted breads, custom celebration cakes, and pastries that tell the story of quality ingredients meeting time-honored techniques.
