Our Story

Born from a passion for creating fresh, affordable baked goods in Naivasha, Kahaki Bakers began as a small start-up dream in a modest market stall in 2021.

Today, we’re proud to serve Naivasha and beyond with daily loaves and pastries that tell the story of ambition, resilience, and community spirit.
